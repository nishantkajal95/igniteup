About IgniteUP

IgniteUP is a professional consulting firm focused on advising Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) across Haryana on government subsidies, financial structuring, risk management, and business visibility.

We operate with a documentation-first, compliance-driven approach similar to established CA and legal advisory firms, ensuring that every engagement is handled with procedural accuracy, transparency, and accountability.

Our Philosophy

Effective consulting for MSMEs requires more than awareness of schemes or finance options. It requires a clear understanding of regulatory frameworks, attention to documentation, realistic eligibility assessment, and ethical advisory practices.

Our Experience

IgniteUP has advised and supported 20+ MSMEs across Haryana, facilitating more than ₹35 Crore in government subsidies through structured and compliant processes.
