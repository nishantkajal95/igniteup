MSME Subsidy Assistance

IgniteUP provides structured advisory and facilitation services for government subsidies applicable to Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in Haryana.

Our approach is compliance-driven, documentation-focused, and aligned with practices followed by reputed CA and legal advisory firms.

What We Do

  • Eligibility assessment for applicable schemes
  • Interpretation of central and Haryana government subsidies
  • Documentation review and application support
  • Departmental coordination and follow-ups
  • Disbursement-stage advisory support

Important Note

Subsidies are subject to statutory eligibility, procedural compliance, and approvals by concerned authorities. IgniteUP does not guarantee subsidy outcomes.
