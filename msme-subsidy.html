Professional Subsidy Advisory

IgniteUP provides structured advisory and facilitation support for eligible Central and Haryana Government subsidy schemes applicable to Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs).

Our approach is compliance-driven and documentation-focused, aligned with the standards followed by reputed CA and legal advisory firms.

Our Support Includes

Eligibility Assessment

Identification of applicable subsidy schemes based on business profile and location.

Scheme Interpretation

Clear explanation of benefit structure, caps, timelines, and compliance requirements.

Documentation Structuring

Guidance on preparing accurate and compliant application documentation.

Departmental Coordination

Professional follow-ups and process guidance during review and disbursement stages.

Important Considerations

Subsidy outcomes are subject to statutory eligibility, procedural compliance, and approval by relevant government authorities. IgniteUP provides advisory and facilitation support but does not guarantee subsidy sanction.
